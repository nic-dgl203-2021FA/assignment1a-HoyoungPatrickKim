Work Experience

I started my first offical part-time job about a month ago. Before then, I volunteered in several different places to get a grasp of working part time jobs plus adding hours to my resume and meet my graduation requirements.

Stocker

Garlic & Pepper Eatery

2021 July - 2021 July

Stocking products whenever the products are coming in, plus helped my managers whenever available such as food preparation, taking orders, dishwashing, and so on. There are two reasons why I had a job here. First, I had been looking for a full/part time job after April since my first year was over so I tried to make money and learn some skills. However, it was not as easy as I thought it would be. I had been applying several places, but did not get any calls or messages from them. Therefore, I asked my mom to see if any of her friends looking for someone to work for them. And I got an opportunity at Garlic & Pepper Eatery to work part time! Another reason that I worked there was I needed money for travelling. At that time, I was planned to go on a road trip with friends in mid July, and I did not have much money in my bank account, so I had to make some money to use for travelling.

Stocker

Cascadia Liquor Store Courtenay

2021 August - Present

Breaking down products on skids plus putting them into cooler. Sometimes, I do duties such as filling up free ice bags, sweeping floors, and recycling whenever the store is going slow or getting close to closing. I became one of the members here because it had clean and wholesome environment to work. All the staffs are kind and considerate. Moreover, a close friend of mine's mom knows the manager well and she gave a good recommendation of me to work there. How lucky am I! I am very happy to work there and I am glad I got my first part-time job in Canada.
